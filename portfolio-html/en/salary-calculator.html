A mobile app that simplifies salary calculation for shift workers dealing with overtime, night pay, holiday pay, and organization-specific rules — removing the need to manually reconstruct payroll logic every month.

The Problem

Shift-based salary structures are hard to calculate manually — each workplace applies different combinations of night shifts, overtime, and holiday rules with no standard formula.

The Solution

Customizable pay rules, shift pattern handling, and reporting so workers understand their payroll without rebuilding calculations from scratch every cycle.

  • Rule-driven pay calculation for real conditions
  • Shift pattern handling and monthly reporting
  • Premium feature structure with monetization logic
